FAQ

Can you remove bad reviews?

No. We cannot remove truthful reviews. What we do instead is help you respond professionally, encourage more positive reviews from real customers, and monitor new feedback so issues are handled quickly.

What does “review management” include?

We send automated review requests to your customers by SMS or email, track new reviews across major platforms, and notify you when action is needed.

What is Google Business Profile (GBP) management?

We set up or clean up your Google listing, keep business details accurate, add photos and services, and post updates to keep your profile active.

What does “monthly reporting” mean?

You receive a simple monthly summary showing how many new reviews you received, your average rating, and what changed during the month.

What does “posts & optimization” include?

We publish regular Google posts and adjust categories, descriptions, and settings to improve visibility in local search results.

What does “cancel anytime” mean?

There are no long-term contracts. You can stop service at the end of any billing month with no penalties.
